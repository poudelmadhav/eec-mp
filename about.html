About Us

Everest Educational Consultancy(EEC) is a leading educational consultancy company in Nepal which offers and provides a variety of services to students looking for various options to study abroad, including but not limited to, procuring admission in leading universities/colleges all over the world through it office (s).

We provide support to individuals, corporate organizations and scholarship bodies who desire quality academic and professional development overseas. Our organization has experience Staff that have worked in organizations such as United Kingdom Education Advisory Service (UKEAS), Dave Abion Consultancy just to mention a few, with 4 – 8 years working experience in this industry before joining us. You can be sure of our excellent performance in the area of student’s recruitment and visa counseling.

At present, we represent and recruit students for foundation, undergraduate, post graduate and doctorate degree programs for Top ranking higher institutions in the United Kingdom (UK), United States of America (USA), Australia, etc. EEC record 100% success rate in its admissions and Visa services; students with lower credit at HND, third class and second class lower grades could be assisted to gain admission into standard schools and institutions of high repute in any of the destinations mentioned above.

Our operation is guided by a very high level of professionalism, integrity and customer satisfaction. Our success could be attributed to the personal relationship our teeming clientele enjoy from our experience, intelligent and efficient professionals assist in providing a hitch free application process, Visa counseling, pre-departure briefing and accommodation services.
